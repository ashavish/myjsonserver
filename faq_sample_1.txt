Do I have to return the free gift received along with the product while returning the product?
- As per the purchase history of this order, the free gift is an inclusive item of the originally delivered product and hence will need to be returned. 
- Please note that if you want to return the product then it should be new, unused, and not tested as the same can be identified in the quality check.

What if I used discount vouchers or loyalty points during time of payment and I have to cancel my order?
- Discount vouchers are intended for one-time use only and shall be treated as used even if you cancel the order. 
- If you had redeemed loyalty points for an order, the same will be credited back to your ABC account within 7-9 business days does not include Saturday, Sunday, and Bank holidays.

I paid for Cash on Delivery, how would I get the refund?
- For payments made via cash on delivery, we can refund the money back to your ABC wallet, Bank account, UPI ID. Please select your preferred mode of refund while creating the return request.
